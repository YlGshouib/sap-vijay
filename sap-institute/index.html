The SAP EAM (Plant Maintenance) module covers:
• Organizational Structure & Master Data

Enhanced Work Clearance Management (WCM) is a critical safety module in SAP EAM that manages:
• Safety permits and work approvals
• Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures
• Isolation management for equipment maintenance
• Safety certificate processing
• Integration with operational processes
• Compliance with industrial safety standards
This is especially important for industries like Oil & Gas, Power, Chemicals, and Manufacturing.
